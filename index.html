    🚀 What METAFOOT is building – a football universe where everyone can earn

    You’re mining $FOOT today. But tomorrow, you’ll use it to be part of something much bigger.
    Here’s what’s coming – and why you should be excited.

    1. 🏠 Rental system – earn even when you’re not playing

    · Own a team? Rent it out to another player for a fee in $FOOT. You keep the fee – that’s your passive income.
    · The renter now controls your team. They can enter PvP matches and tournaments.
    · When the renter wins prize money: It’s split 50/50 between the renter and the team’s players (the avatars signed to that team). The team owner already got paid – no extra split.

    Why miners will love this:
    · Team owners earn without playing.
    · Renters can play and earn without owning a team.
    · Players (avatars) – that’s you – earn a share of every win, even if the team is rented.

    2. 🧠 Train player – you are the player

    Every time you train your avatar (speed, shot, power, intelligence), you’re building your digital football self.
    The stronger you become, the more managers will want to sign you to their team.

    Think of it as your career in the metaverse.

    3. 💰 How you earn – fair for everyone

    In real football, players sign contracts and get paid when the team wins. Same here.
    · When a manager wins a tournament: 70% of the prize goes to all the players signed to that team (shared equally). 30% goes to the manager.
    · Example: A manager wins 1,000,000 $FOOT. The team players share 700,000 $FOOT. You get your cut. The manager keeps 300,000.
    · In a PvP match (rented team): The renter and the team players split winnings 50/50. The team owner already received their rental fee – no extra split.

    All rules are enforced by smart contracts – no cheating.
    Why this is huge: You earn passively just by being signed to a winning team. The more you train, the more valuable you become.

    4. 🔁 Buy and sell player – only managers, like real football

    If you choose Manager Mode, you can buy and sell player avatars on the market. You might make a profit or a loss – just like real football transfers. This creates a living economy inside the game.

    Managers enter tournaments, play matches, and win trophies and $FOOT prizes. The better your team (and the players you sign), the more you can win.

    · $FOOT ELITE LEAGUE – the Champions League. The ultimate prize.
